About the project MERCURY is a production of InfoAmazonia, a network of journalists who investigate the main environmental issues in the nine countries of the Amazon. The investigation was conducted in four countries during one year. The work was done in partnership with journalists from Armando Info in Venezuela and Fantástico (TV Globo) in Brazil. Along with this special platform, we are launching a documentary directed by Tom Laffay The entire production was supported by the Rainforest Journalism Fund of the Pulitzer Center and IUCN (World Conservation Union) The Netherlands. Versions of the stories were also published in Knack (Belgium), Stabroek News (Guyana), De Ware Tijd and UnitedNews.sr (Suriname), besides the stories by our partners in Brazil (Link) and Venezuela (Link)
